I created this website for my SI 539 class at the School of Information at the University of Michigan. SI 539 is titled "Web Design: Responsiveness and Accessibility." I took this class my first semester of completing my Master of Science degree in Information. A co-requisite for this course is SI 506. SI 506 is a class about coding in Python.

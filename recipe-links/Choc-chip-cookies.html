The Best Chocolate Chip Cookies

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together 1/2 cup granulated sugar, 1/2 cup light brown sugar, packed, 1/4 cup dark brown sugar, 1 teaspoon salt, and 1/2 cup melted unsalted butter together until a paste forms with no lumps
  2. Whisk in 1 egg and 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  3. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  4. Sift in the 1 ¼ cups all-purpose flour and 1/2 teaspoon baking soda, then fold the mixture with a spatula
  5. Fold in as many Semi-Sweet Chocolate Morsels as your heart desires. If I using a 12 oz bag of chocolate morsels, I usually pour in 1/3 of the bag first and then add more if I feel like it.
  6. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  7. Scoop the dough with a spoon onto the parchment paper-lined baking sheet, leaving at least 4 inches of space between cookies and 2 inches of space from the edges of the pan so that the cookies can spread evenly.
  8. Bake for 12-15 minutes
  9. Tip: It's going to look runny and not cooked. That's ok. It'll continue to cook and harden on the pan. Don't worry.
  10. Cool completely before serving. Then enjoy!
